Hardwares provided for Surveillance system



  • IP Bullet
    The bullet camera can be used both outdoor and indoor, depend on the type that will be used. But, the bullet camera use fixed camera, so it can capture footage only in specific corner.
    Type of Samsung CCTV for example: SNO-L6013R and QNO-6010R

  • Dome
    Dome Camera is type that commonly used by people. There are some variations in dome type, fix camera (camera can only monitor in one direction) and speed dome (camera can rotate fast).
    Type of Samsung CCTV for example: SND-L6013R and QND-6010R

  • Box
    Box camera commonly used both for indoor and outdoor. The advantage of box camera is that a box camera is able to produce good quality images, although in minimum lighting condition.
    Type of Samsung CCTV for example: SNB-5004

  • PTZ
    PTZ (Pan Tilt Zoom) camera has the ability to move up and down, turn left and turn right, and to zoom pictures up to several zooming number. This camera can be used to monitor large area, to facilitate the CCTV supervisor in monitoring.
    Samsung: SNP-L5233H and SNP-L6233H plus controller SPC 2000

  • Fisheye
    Fisheye CCTV use panoramic camera that can produce better quality of images with 180 degree of viewing angle. IP camera with fisheye lense can be remotely monitored through PC, laptop, and smartphone.
    Samsung SNF-7010

  • DVR or NVR
    The device that used as server and storage, to store the footages from all cameras that connected to this device. The number of DVR or NVR channel also varies, e.g. 4,8, 16, 32, and 64 channels. This number of channel means the maximum number of camera that can be connected to NVR/DVR
    Samsung: NVR 16 (SRN-1673S) and 64 channel

In common, the characteristics of cctv cameras are divided into 2 types:

  • Weatherproof camera

    The waterproof camera is designed to prevent water from rain, snow, fog, and tiny things like dirt or dust that can damage the internal component of camera. Physical design of weatherproof camera is designed to avoid bad and extreme weather, and also to make reliable monitoring in every condition.

  • Explosionproof camera

    This type of camera usually used in dangerous area with the high risks. For example, in factory, oil refinery, or mining area that may be happen an explosion. The camera is designed to withstand any explosions that can happen in area with extreme condition. Also, the camera is equipped with the latest technology of dust and waterproof.
