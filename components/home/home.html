WHO WE ARE

MSS was founded in 2009 as a IT Company. With an initial focus on Dedicated Server and Enterprise Email Service, we entered the network infrastructure market in 2014. Today, we are a full-cycle service provider with full-time professionals covering all areas of IT. Our team members bring their unique set of skills to our departments: web development, mobile development, network infrastructure and technical service.



WHAT WE DO

We build exceptional web and mobile applications, business approached solutions, and reliable IT infrastructure. These services flawlessly align with client objectives, solving simple or complex business problems. Our team is always ready to take on projects of any size, able to start from scratch or enhance and build on existing. With professional expertise and a thoroughly organized development process, we can help clients overcome any and every technical challenge to make their IT dreams a reality.
